How it works

Four steps. No human in the loop.

Every venue, framing and final image is chosen by code — the pipeline decides what’s good enough to send.

🌱

Discover

Pull independent cafés, salons & restaurants from Google Places, filter out chains automatically.

📷

Capture

Re-aim Street View at the venue’s own doorway with bearing math; a vision model checks the framing.

🌼

Compose

Place the client’s real planters into the photo at door-anchored scale — nothing else altered.

✓

Vet

A strict QA gate rejects anything unfaithful or unrealistic. Fail closed — never ship a bad image.

Automated judgement
